How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the Security section in your account settings and select "Change Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawal processing times vary by method. Bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days, while cryptocurrency withdrawals are usually processed within 30 minutes.
What are the trading fees?
Our trading fees start at 0.25% for makers and 0.35% for takers. Fees decrease based on your 30-day trading volume and Bluqq token holdings.
How do I enable two-factor authentication?
You can enable 2FA in the Security section of your account settings. We recommend using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator for the best security.
Is my account information secure?
Yes, we use bank-level security encryption and storage methods to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
