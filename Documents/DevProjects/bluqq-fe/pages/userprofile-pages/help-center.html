Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the Security & Privacy section in your account settings and select "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and then create a new one. Make sure your new password is strong and unique.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing times vary depending on the method. Bank transfers ate instant , while crypto withdrawals are usually processed within 30 minutes. P2P transactions are instant.

What should I do if my transaction is stuck?

If your transaction is stuck, first check the transaction status in your history. For crypto transactions, you can check the blockchain explorer using the provided TXID. If the issue persists, contact our support team with the transaction details.

How do I enable 2FA?

You can enable two-factor authentication in the Security & Privacy section. We recommend using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy for the best security. You'll need to scan a QR code and enter a verification code to complete setup.
